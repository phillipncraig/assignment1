Landmark Development History

  • Falcon 1 Flight 4 — September 28, 2008

    The first privately funded liquid-fueled rocket to reach orbit

  • Falcon 9 Flight 2 — December 9, 2010

    The first privately funded company to successfully launch, orbit, and recover a spacecraft

  • Falcon 9 Flight 3 — May 25, 2012

    The first private company to send a spacecraft to the International Space Station

  • Falcon 9 Flight 7 — December 3, 2013

    The first private company to send a satellite into geosynchronous orbit

  • Falcon 9 Flight 20 — December 22, 2015

    TThe first landing of an orbital rocket's first stage on land

  • Falcon 9 Flight 23 — April 8, 2016

    The first landing of an orbital rocket's first stage on an ocean platform

  • Falcon 9 Flight 32 — March 30, 2017

    The first relaunch and landing of a used orbital rocket

  • Falcon 9 Flight 32 — March 30, 2017

    The first controlled flyback and recovery of a payload fairing

  • Falcon 9 Flight 35 — June 3, 2017

    The first reflight of a commercial cargo spacecraft.
